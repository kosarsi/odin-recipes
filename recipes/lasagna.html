Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is one of the oldest and most popular types of pasta. It consists of very wide, flat sheets stacked together with meat in between the sheets.

Lasagna contains things like tomato sauce, beef, meatballs, and cheese.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato Products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasoning
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
