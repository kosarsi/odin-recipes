Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Chocolate chip cookies are a popular dessert. They are a type of cookie that contains many chocolate chips.

Chocolate chip cookies usually are made with flour, eggs, chocolate chips, and other ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla
  • Baking soda
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Flour
  • Chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Beat the butter and sugars, then beat in the eggs and vanilla
  2. Dissolve the baking soda in hot water and add to the mixture
  3. Stir in the flour and chocolate chips
  4. Drop dough onto a prepared baking sheet
  5. Bake until the edges are golden brown
